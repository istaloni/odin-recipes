Beef Nilaga

Description

Beef Nilaga is a comforting Filipino soup made with tender beef, usually brisket or shank, simmered with various vegetables like potatoes, corn, cabbage, and sometimes green beans. The broth is clear and flavorful, often seasoned simply with salt and pepper, allowing the natural flavors of the meat and vegetables to shine. It's a hearty dish, perfect for cold weather, and is often enjoyed with rice. The name "nilaga" means "boiled," highlighting its straightforward cooking method that results in a warm, nourishing meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef neck with bones
  • Onion
  • Peppercorn
  • Bay Leaf
  • Fish Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Beef Broth
  • Potato
  • Saging na saba
  • BokChoy
  • Cabbage

Steps

  1. Boil Beef neck in boiling water for 1 hour.
  2. During the first 30 minutes of boiling, remove scrum using a strainer.
  3. After removing the scrum, chopped onions, pepper corn, and bay leaves.
  4. Boil for 30 more minutes.
  5. Add beef broth and beef cubes.
  6. Add chopped potatoes. Boil for 10 mins.
  7. Add saging na saba. Boil for 10 mins.
  8. Add Bokchoy and cabbage. Boil for 10 mins.
  9. Add salt, pepper, and fish sauce to taste.
